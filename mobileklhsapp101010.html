Welcome to North Dakota's Original High School Radio Program. Linton High School is Happy to be leading the way in the field of Radio Broadcasting in the State of North Dakota. Our High School Program has been in existence 2 1/2 Years and already has received 5 Golden Microphone Awards from the Intercollegiate Broadcast System and Letters of Congratulations from our Senators and Congressmen.
